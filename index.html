STOP Touching Your Face

STOP Touching Your Face sends you a notification when you touch your face to help prevent the spread of COVID-19.

In doing so, your webcam images are processed locally on your machine and at no time is any information collected or sent externally.

Download now to STOP Touching Your Face!



Features🚀

🤚

Image classifier to detect if a user is touching their face



🔔

Snooze notification allows you to set a minimum time required before the app can send another notificatio when touching your face



🔋

Low power mode activates when the webcam is off allowing for notifications to be sent out periodically
